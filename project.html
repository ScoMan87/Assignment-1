Project Name: Asisten

A problem that is apparent within out aging community is that of security, safety, and wellbeing. Our aged communities are at extra vulnerability when it comes to their safety and security. Asisten is a tool generated to assisting those in aged care (and their families) to obtain information and tool sets to be able to be informed and protected. Built in conjunction with aged care advocacy groups:

Asisten aims to help identify human rights.

Identifying and protecting themselves from elder abuse, as well as procedures on what to do if they suspect such behavior or fall victim.

Identifying and protecting against illicit activities such as internet scams.

Asisten also has a live chat function that anyone can raise a question before making any decisions they are faced.
